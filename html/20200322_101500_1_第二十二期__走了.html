“

看似战胜了鼠疫，忘却了曾经所有悲痛，忘却了那些从同一辆火车上下来，却没有找到亲人的人。

······

对那些母亲、丈夫、妻子或者爱人来说，他们的一切快乐随着亲人被埋进无名的土坑或者化为一堆灰烬而远去，对他们来说，鼠疫还在那里。


但谁又会考虑那些孤独的人呢？

——《鼠疫》 加缪

”
走了
Gone

作者：艾晓明
Xiaoming AI
Translated by：Brandon

武汉的田先生说

照片上左三是小区邻居

前天走了

上有老下有小

左四是他的妻子

女儿与我的大孙子同龄

“The third from the left stands

My neighbor who’s gone the day before

With young and old to take care

Beside him the wife holding hands

To his daughter who shares

My grandson’s age”

At the photo a man sighed

Mr. Tian from Wuhan


武汉的王先生说

我高中的班主任走了

夏天时我还去探望过他

他请我们吃了饭

又把我送到路口

至今记得他说

这么热的天

你不该来的

“He’s gone

My high school headteacher

A visit paid to him just last summer

A meal treated to us as generous return

A walk of farewell I still remember

In his parting word

‘Fiercely the world burns

You should have never come’”

In deep sorrow a student mourned

Mr. Wang from Wuhan


那位灵车后嚎啕的女人说

琴琴啊 你的爸爸就这样走了啊

你没有爸爸了

“QinQin, my hon

 QinQin, my hon

Daddy has gone

He has so gone

To whom you will never belong”

Miserable was a woman

Wailing and kneeling

Right behind a coffin


仙桃的刘文雄医生走了

1月21日 他看了180位病人

一个月里 他接诊3181人

每天晚上还要接电话问诊

一个月只休息了两天

第三天因为胸痛去了医院

Doctor Liu from Xiantao has gone

On the day of January 21th

180 patients were received by him alone

The number soared to 3181

Within only a single month

While phone calls of inquiry bombarded after shifts were done

Two days was how long he sorely took a breath

The day that followed witnessed a doctor got on a sickbed

For there was a killing pain in the chest


夏医生走了 留下两岁的孩子

她满月一样的脸庞让我想起小时候

终年29 为什么是29

无解的数字和哀伤的永恒

Doctor Xia has gone

Leaving her two-year-old baby alone

Whose waxed round face

Reminds me of my childhood

Aged 29  but how in God’s name come

A number beyond reason

A grief beyond time


很远很远的一个养蜂人

也撇下他的蜂群走了

蜜蜂在花间翩飞

蜂蜜已封装入瓶

大路空旷 他就是不等了

From a far distance

A beekeeper abandoned his swarm

Because he has gone

In flowers bees are dancing around

Honey has been bottled well done

The road ahead is wide open

Yet going wither he is bent


家家走的时候说

对不起 姑娘

你要找个好人嫁啊

不想拖累你们

“Forgive me, my little lady

Find a good man to marry

I shall not slow you down”

Said grandma, who was about to have gone


爷爷走的时候

啥话也没有留下

高烧燃尽了体内的病毒

死因写的是 帕金森

Whereas grandpa has gone

Ain’t no words left to bond

Fever so high

That virus got burned to the ground

On the acquaintance form

Parkinson was written down


有个爸爸悄悄走了

手机里有一串求助记录

我发烧了 我发烧了

社区回复 没办法啊

没有医院接收啊

爸爸走的时候

不带门钥匙

Quietly a father has gone

Piles of phone records to be found:

“Fever is on  Fever is on”

“Our hands are tied

Ain’t no hospitals to be found”

The local community had so replied

Finally a bed for patients is supplied

Dad was leaving with the key behind


也有的人不想走

被拖起来拖到楼下

我听见一位男人一路在喊

让我把电视关了

让我把门关了

让我把手机拿倒

唉 你怎么打人啊

打他的人在按他的头

要他蹲倒戴口罩

Someone was not leaving

Only to be dragged downstairs

Heard a man all the way crying

“My TV is playing

My door ain’t closing

My phone is missing

For Christ’s sake, why you hitting?”

The hitter pressed his head down

Compelling to wear his mask on


每一天我都听说有人走了

我祈祷 不要是我认识的人

你不要这样不够朋友

说走就走 留下这个春天

芳草萋萋鹦鹉洲

When I hear someone’s gone every day

“not the ones I know” I pray

That’s not how friendship works—

Not even a message, gone without delay

Deserting a spring where wild grass grows


还有那个懂事的孩子

给猝死的爷爷盖好被子

我希望有人带他走

走得远远的

去那个被称为美的国

每当中国孤儿到达

会有很多父母来接

And that thoughtful kid

Who tucks his sudden-death grandpa well in bed

I pray for someone taking him away

Away to the Promise Land, for which we pled

Every time orphans of China get on the web

Plenty of parents come to fill the form


春天在这里走来走去

鬼魂在这里转转悠悠

有人在传布领钱的消息

有人在路边烧钱纸

Right here

Springs come and go

Spirits haunt to and fro

Rumor has it

That there’s money to get

The fact is by the roadside

That there’s offering to give

Burning paper is the way


给走了的人烧

也给新冠病毒烧吧

屡屡青烟就是你的心愿

走了的人都能听见

Burn it for the ones who have gone

Burn it for the Coronavirus down to none

Dust to Dust 

Ashes to Ashes

Everything rests in peace

May the dead hear our wish


花开时你要回来饮酒

记得分发你的喜糖

与来不及告别的人一一拥抱

抱一下武汉 你的乡愁

In the season when flowers bloom

Remember to come back home

Go on fetching shots and binges

Hand out your wedding candies

Hug every one of those whom

You didn’t get to wave goodbye to

A hug for Wuhan

The scar forever grows on your mind


February 26th 2020


*注：本文封面图选自《人物》2月29日报道《在武汉街头流浪》
